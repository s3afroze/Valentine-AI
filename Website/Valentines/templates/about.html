What is Valentines AI?

It is the only AI that can generate romantic quotes. Who knows, AI generated lines might be the key to one of the most difficult problems in history of mankind– human emotions?

Author

The project was created by Shahzeb Afroze, co-founder of Algo Consulting. Connect with me on Linkedin

Vision:

The vision is to apply machine learning to enable creativity. Generating ideas are difficult but what if AI could give us a headstart on it?

AI is blurring the defination of artist (one of the most creative careers). This is shown time and time again in the recent years, "In 2018 Mario Klingemann won a Lumen prize for his art piece The Butcher’s Son, a set of Francis Bacon-like images." - The Guardian
